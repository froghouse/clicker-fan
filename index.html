Welcome to Clicker Fan!

Ever dreamt of becoming a huge artist, but never really had the time to pick up a guitar and learn the ropes?

Not to worry - with Clicker Fan you can instead waste that time by clicking the browser window over and over again! Not only will this grant you sweet satisfaction and fulfillment; you will also gain virtual fans in the process!

Click the artist avatar in the middle of the screen to begin attempting to impress fans. The faster you click, the quicker you'll impress them! New upgrades and powerups are unlocked once you reach certain amounts of fame, and you can then have your fans influence your career and increase the rate in which you impress new fans (even passively!)

Happy clicking!
